Welcome to SavitaSoft Computer Education

SavitaSoft is an institute dedicated to teaching Core Computer Technologies to students and professionals. Established in the year 1995, the institute has had the opportunity of teaching thousands of students and professional.

The teaching philosophy deployed, strives to create in-depth knowledge about the subject at hand. We believe that depth is essential ingredient to achieve heights in software development. Students from SavitaSoft have proved the point by their work in industry worldwide. The key to our students success comes from our small classes and flexible schedules, giving students the options and time necessary to learn.
